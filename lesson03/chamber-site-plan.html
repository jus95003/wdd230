Site Plan for the

Queen Creek

Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

Queen Creek Chamber of Commerce

This name represents the city in which the chamber operates.

Site Purpose

The Queen Creek Chamber of Commerce is focused on connecting the community with local businesses.

The Chamber works to provide resources and relief to its members while keeping the community informed and involved.

Scenarios

How does the chamber advocate for policies that create a better business environment for my business?

How does the chamber support and enhance workforce development within the community?

How can chamber members be connected with each other and the community?
