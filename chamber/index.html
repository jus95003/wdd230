Events

The Queen Creek Holiday Festival & Parade will be held on Saturday, December 2nd from 2PM – 6:30PM in the heart of downtown Queen Creek. This event includes a variety of activities including specialty craft vendors and food, photos with Santa, entertainment, tree lighting, lots of activities for kids, and more! This is a free event to attend, with nominal charges for food. Now in its 41st year, the annual event benefits the Queen Creek Chamber of Commerce and Kiwanis Club of Queen Creek.

Apricot Lane Boutique celebrated their grand opening on Thursday, November 16th. In attendance were Queen Creek Chamber of Commerce staff, local business owners, Queen Creek Council Member Dawn Oliphant, and Apricot Lane Boutique owner Stephanie Neal and staff. Apricot Lane Boutique is located at 20736 E Maya Rd, Suite 104, Queen Creek, AZ 85142.

PIEfection celebrated their grand opening on Friday, November 10th. In attendance were Queen Creek Chamber of Commerce staff, local business owners, Queen Creek Vice Mayor Jeff Brown, Queen Creek Council Member Travis Padilla, and PIEfection owner Cheryl Standage and family. PIEfection is located at 20311 S Ellsworth Rd, Suite 113, Queen Creek, AZ 85142.
